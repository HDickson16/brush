OUR GOAL

Our goal here is to give anyone of any age to learn something new about art!
This company started very small like many often do, but we kept our heads up and look at us now!
Our company is full of fun activities that any age would be interested in, such as.
Painting, Drawing, Pottery, Digital Design, etc.
You can also apply to work with us!
We have flexible schedules, and our main goal is to learn something new!
So if you want to get your creativity working again, stop by Painting Pathway!
